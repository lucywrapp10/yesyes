About

yesyes is a minimal static site scaffold designed to get out of your way. No build step, no framework lock-in — just plain HTML, CSS, and a sprinkle of JavaScript.

Fork it, rename it, restyle it. Everything is kept small and readable so you can shape it to fit whatever you're building.

Features

Fast

Static files load instantly. No bundler required.

Simple

Plain HTML, CSS, and JS you can read top-to-bottom.

Responsive

Works on phones, tablets, and desktops out of the box.

Dark mode

Respects your OS theme automatically.

Accessible

Semantic markup and keyboard-friendly navigation.

Yours

No telemetry, no dependencies — fully editable.
